Mini-Hydro Power Plant Design & Integration with DC Microgrid
(MHPDIMG-2024)

Course Overview

Join us for an international GIAN course on mini-hydro power plant design and integration with DC microgrids. This course aims to impart essential knowledge on developing eco-friendly mini-hydro systems, suitable for integration into microgrids to enhance energy reliability and sustainability.

Course Highlights
  • Expert Lectures, Tutorials, and Hands-On Sessions
  • Focus on Practical Demonstrations & Case Studies
  • International and Local Faculty from Leading Institutions
  • Opportunity to Earn a Certification
